The Founder

In 1578, Fray Juan de Plasencia and Fray Diego de Oropesa, two Franciscan missionaries from Spain founded the town of Tayabas in order to spread Christianity to its natives.

Tayabas Public Market is a marketplace located in Tayabas City. Tayabas City is located in Province of Quezon, Calabarzon, Philippines. Public Market in Tayabas is divide in two, Wet market and Dry market, The wet market is located in second floor and the dry market is in ground floor, We have street food located on the left side of the market, like ihaw-ihaw and Burger.
