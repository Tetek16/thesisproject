About The Barangays



Barangay Angeles Zone-1

Angeles Zone I, formerly Poblacion, is a barangay in the city of Tayabas, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 1,995. This represented 1.77% of the total population of Tayabas.

Barangay Ilayang Bukal

Bukal Ilaya is a barangay in the city of Tayabas, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 784. This represented 0.70% of the total population of Tayabas.



Barangay San Diego Zone-3

San Diego Zone III is a barangay in the city of Tayabas, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 291. This represented 0.26% of the total population of Tayabas.

Barangay San Isidro Zone-4

San Isidro Zone IV is a barangay in the city of Tayabas, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 761. This represented 0.68% of the total population of Tayabas.
